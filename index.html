FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What courses are available at AR Square IT?

We offer 17+ courses including Web Development, App Development, Digital Marketing, Tally Prime, Generative AI, Graphic Design, Video Editing, and many more. All courses include live project work.

Do you offer placement assistance?

Yes! We provide 100% placement assistance including resume building, interview preparation, and job referrals through our industry network in Jaipur and beyond.

What is the duration of courses?

Course durations vary from 1 month (short skill courses like Data Entry, Typing) to 6 months (comprehensive programs like Web Development, App Development). Please contact us for the specific course schedule.

Are the courses available online or offline?

We primarily offer offline, in-person training at our Jaipur center for the best hands-on experience. Select courses may have online options — please contact us for details.

Do I get a certificate after completing a course?

Yes, every student receives an industry-recognized certificate from AR Square IT upon successfully completing their course, which is highly valued by employers across Rajasthan.
